Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

FoodLens is a macro tracking app built for Indian food. This policy explains how the app handles your data.

Data we collect

None. FoodLens does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data.

What stays on your device

Everything you enter — meals, macro targets, body metrics, and preferences — is stored locally on your iPhone using Apple's SwiftData framework. It never leaves your device.

  • Meal logs
  • Daily macro history
  • Profile information (age, weight, height, fitness goal)
  • App preferences

Internet access

FoodLens does not require an internet connection and makes no network requests. There are no accounts, no sign-in, and no servers.

Third-party services

FoodLens uses no third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs.

Data deletion

Deleting the app from your iPhone removes all data permanently. There is no cloud backup to delete separately.

Children

FoodLens does not knowingly collect data from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a new date.
